# Visual Guide - What You'll See

## Enhanced 3D Visualizations

### Procedural Textures Added

#### **Wood Grain Texture**
- **Applied to:** Side tables, console tables, desk surfaces
- **Visual appearance:** Curved grain lines resembling natural wood
- **Color:** Warm brown with organic flowing patterns
- **Creates:** Realistic furniture that looks hand-crafted

#### **Fabric Weave Texture**
- **Applied to:** Sofa cushions, backrest, arms
- **Visual appearance:** Fine textile weave pattern
- **Color:** Taupe/beige with subtle variations
- **Creates:** Cozy, touchable-looking upholstery

#### **Decorative Rug Pattern**
- **Applied to:** Living room floor rug
- **Visual appearance:** Bordered design with dot pattern
- **Color:** Cream with tan border and geometric shapes
- **Creates:** Traditional rug aesthetic

#### **Hardwood Floor**
- **Applied to:** All room floors
- **Visual appearance:** Planked boards with grain lines
- **Color:** Light wood with natural variations
- **Creates:** Realistic hardwood flooring

### Enhanced Flower Arrangements

#### **Before (Simple Spheres)**
- Plain colored balls representing blooms
- No detail or realism
- Hard to identify as flowers

#### **After (Petal Geometry)**
- **Flower Structure:**
  - Golden center sphere (realistic stamen)
  - 5-8 individual petals per bloom
  - Petals arranged in circular pattern
  - Each petal tilted for natural look

- **Colors:**
  - Soft pink (#ffc0cb)
  - Light pink (#ffe4e1)
  - Lavender (#dda0dd)
  - Pale yellow (#f0e68c)

- **Arrangement Details:**
  - Green stems connecting to vase
  - Leaves positioned around stems
  - Flowers at varying heights
  - Natural clustering
