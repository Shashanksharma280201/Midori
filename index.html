Interior Inspiration

Styling Your Space

Expert tips on selecting the perfect planter for your interior aesthetic and plant type

Minimalist Modern

Clean lines and neutral tones create serene spaces. Pair architectural plants with geometric planters in matte finishes.

  • Choose monochromatic color palettes
  • Opt for simple, geometric shapes
  • Consider negative space

Bohemian Eclectic

Mix textures and patterns fearlessly. Terracotta and ceramic in warm earth tones complement lush, trailing greenery.

  • Layer different heights and sizes
  • Embrace handcrafted imperfections
  • Add woven and macram√© accents

Scandinavian Serenity

Light wood tones and white ceramics bring hygge warmth. Keep displays simple and intentional for maximum impact.

  • Focus on quality over quantity
  • Use natural materials
  • Maximize natural light exposure
