Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I book a consultation?

Simply fill out our contact form or call us directly. We'll schedule a convenient time to visit your space (or discuss via video call) to understand your vision, measure the area, assess lighting, and discuss your preferences. Consultations are always complimentary.

What's included in the placement service?

Our placement experts consider lighting angles, sight lines, color harmony with your existing decor, and optimal viewing positions. We physically place the arrangement, adjust for perfect positioning, and ensure everything is stable and safe. Think of it as floral interior design.

How long do arrangements last?

With proper care, our arrangements typically last 7-14 days depending on the flowers used. We source only the freshest blooms and provide detailed care instructions. Many clients opt for our weekly or bi-weekly refresh service to maintain continuous beauty.

Do you provide vases, or do I need my own?

All our packages include a carefully selected vase that complements both the flowers and your space. We have relationships with artisan ceramicists and source vessels specifically for each arrangement. The vase is yours to keep.

Can you work within a specific color palette?

Absolutely! During consultation, we discuss your color preferences, existing decor, and the mood you want to create. We can work within specific palettes, match brand colors for corporate clients, or create seasonal themes. Customization is our specialty.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the greater metropolitan area within a 50-mile radius. For premium and bespoke clients, we're happy to discuss extended service areas. Corporate contracts and special events may qualify for travel to additional locations.
